The Founder

Alvin Yap

Founder & Marketing Strategist, ADV Digital Marketing

Alvin Yap is a performance marketing strategist and the founder of ADV Digital Marketing, a full-service digital marketing agency based in Kuala Lumpur. With expertise spanning paid media, marketing automation, search engine optimisation, and AI-powered marketing systems, Alvin works with Malaysian businesses to build compounding marketing infrastructure — not just individual campaigns.

Alvin's approach is defined by systems thinking: every tactic is connected to attribution, every spend is tied to measurable return, and every client engagement is structured to improve over time. ADV operates on a profit-sharing model for select clients, aligning the agency's success directly with business growth outcomes.

ADV Digital Marketing is Alvin's primary platform for advancing AI-native marketing practices in Malaysia — including Answer Engine Optimisation (AEO) for ChatGPT and Perplexity, and Generative Engine Optimisation (GEO) for Google AI Overviews.
