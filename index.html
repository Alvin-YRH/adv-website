AEO (Answer Engine Optimisation) structures your content so AI tools like ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google Gemini, and Apple Intelligence can accurately extract and cite your business. Both matter because a growing share of discovery now happens through AI rather than traditional search. ADV implements AEO and GEO through FAQ schema markup, entity definition content, and Schema.org JSON-LD structured data.
