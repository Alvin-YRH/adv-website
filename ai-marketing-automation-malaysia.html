Real Workflow Example

Content brief generation:
step by step.

This is a live workflow ADV runs for clients — from intake to finished brief, in under 8 minutes.

01

Client fills intake form

Target audience, product or service details, campaign keywords, and tone of voice. Structured input that Claude can reason from.

02

Make.com triggers Claude

The intake response fires a Make.com scenario that constructs a structured prompt and passes it to Claude's API with campaign context built in.

03

Claude generates the brief

Full content brief produced in under 8 minutes — hooks, angles, audience pain points, key messages, format recommendations, and CTA direction.

04

Brief lands in Notion

Make.com writes the output directly into the Notion content database, organised by campaign, tagged by platform, and assigned to the right team member.
