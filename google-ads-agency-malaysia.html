Full-funnel keyword mapping. Not just broad match. We build keyword architecture across awareness, consideration, and conversion stages — so every stage of your buyer's journey is covered without cannibalising budget.

PMAX vs Search balance. In the Malaysian market, full PMAX reliance often dilutes intent-rich Search traffic. We calibrate the budget split based on your conversion data, not Google's defaults.

RM3,000/month is our recommended minimum. Less than that and the data is too thin for meaningful optimisation. Below that threshold, the algorithm doesn't get enough conversion signals to make intelligent bid decisions, and you'll spend more time in limbo than in growth. A higher starting budget shortens the learning phase and accelerates results.
Different intent. Google captures active searchers (high intent) — people already looking for what you sell. Meta builds demand (broader audience) — it puts your brand in front of people who don't know they need you yet. Most businesses need both for a complete funnel. If you can only choose one, lead with Google Ads for bottom-of-funnel conversions, then add Meta once you have budget for upper-funnel demand generation.
We structure PMAX asset groups by product or service category, monitor search term reports closely, and use negative keyword lists to prevent budget waste in early phases. PMAX is powerful but can burn budget on irrelevant placements without tight governance. We treat PMAX as a complement to Search — not a replacement — and calibrate the balance based on your specific customer journey and market maturity.
Google scores your keyword/ad/landing page relevance on a 1–10 scale. Higher Quality Score means lower cost-per-click for the same ad position — which means more conversions for the same spend. We optimise all three layers: keyword match and intent alignment, ad copy relevance and CTR, and landing page experience including load speed and message match. A Quality Score improvement from 4 to 7 can reduce your CPC by 30–40% on competitive keywords.
Full management. We own the day-to-day: bid adjustments, search term monitoring, A/B testing of ad copy and landing pages, negative keyword lists, audience exclusions, and weekly reporting with plain-English commentary. You'll have full visibility into the account at all times, and we'll walk you through every significant change we make and why.
Search campaigns typically show conversion data within 2–3 weeks, once tracking is verified and enough impressions have accumulated. PMAX needs 4–6 weeks of learning phase before optimising — Google's algorithm requires a minimum of 30–50 conversion events before Smart Bidding becomes reliable. We set clear expectations for each phase: data collection, early optimisation, and scaled performance.
